Habibie Ainun 3

Producer: Manoj Punjabi

Production: MD Pictures

Cast: Maudy Ayunda, Basmalah Gralind, Reza Rahadian, Jefri Nichol, Lukman Sardi, Marcella Zalianty, Arswendy Bening Swara, Jennifer Coppen, Rebecca Klopper, Teuku Ryzki, Eric Febrian, Aghniny Haque, Arya Saloka, Kevin Ardilova, Diandra Agatha, Angga Aldi Yunanda, Jourdy Pranata, Anodya Shula Neona Ayu, Irsyadillah, Carmela van der Kruk, Mike Lucock, Tegar Satrya, Amaranggana, Alia Miranti, Rassya Hidayah, Haura Lathifa, Graciella Abigail, Stani Arifasti

Description: "Habibie & Ainun 3" is a continuation of Rudy Habibie (2016), a prequel to Habibie & Ainun, and the third film in the Habibie & Ainun series. While Rudy Habibie tells the story of a young Habibie, Habibie & Ainun 3 focuses on a young Ainun. Maudy Ayunda stars as the lead, Reza Rahadian returns to reprise his role, and Jefri Nichol joins the cast as a new addition to the series. It received positive reactions from both audiences and critics.
