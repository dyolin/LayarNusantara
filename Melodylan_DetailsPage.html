Description: Melody transfers schools to escape her troubled past, including her history with ex-boyfriend David. At her new school, she meets Dylan, a cool yet rebellious student admired for his looks. Overwhelmed by the drama, Melody tries to distance herself from Dylan by getting closer to Fathur.
